Beginnings

I started programming in middle school. When COVID-19 forced a lockdown, I was bored out of my mind. It wasn't programming but actually environment design at first. Just doing things in Roblox Studio and finishing a donut tutorial on Blender, I figured that it wasn't quite my thing. Eventually, I got into programming with Luau through Roblox. More tinkering around, browsing the developer forum for far too long, and came upon the small OSS community there. Not only did I find the burning passion that has kept me up many nights, but also a community I felt a part of. The rest was history.
