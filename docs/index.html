If you’re bored, you can read a bit about me below. TLDR; I like making things.

My story begins in middle school, when COVID-19 forced a lockdown. Bored out of my mind, I started exploring. At first environment art, I built things in Roblox Studio, it was fun, but it didn’t feel right. I never was an artist, couldn’t draw, imagine intricate designs, but still I kept going because I loved making things.

Eventually, I got into programming with Luau through Roblox. Programming stuck out to me, it involved problem solving, logic, and creativity while not requiring artistic skill. Kind of like solving a math problem, it was satisfying to see pieces fall into place. I didn’t know it at the time, but this love make me who I am and keep me up many nights.

Moving into high school, it allowed a lot of opportunities for growth and exploration.
